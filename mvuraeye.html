MvuraEye – Site Plan

Site Name

MvuraEye — A name derived from "Mvura" (Shona for water) and "Eye" symbolizing monitoring. It represents a system that monitors the safety and quality of water in Zimbabwean rivers.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a central hub to showcase the MvuraEye project. It will explain the technology behind the system, raise awareness about water safety, display real-time or sample water data, and highlight community impact stories. It targets NGOs, donors, communities, and policy makers.

Scenarios

  • How does MvuraEye detect and report contamination in rivers near mining zones?
  • How can our community request to be included in the MvuraEye monitoring program?

Color Schema

  • Deep Teal (#005f73) – Used for headers and call-to-action buttons.
  • Sky Blue (#94d2bd) – Used for backgrounds and highlights.
